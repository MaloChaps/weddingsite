Today I take you to the ferret for the civil marriage of Charles & Margaux.

A wedding between friends just sublime. Charles and Margaux were married civilly at the town hall of the Canon in the Cap Ferret coronation. This wedding was just magical and very cool. I was just there to shoot a photo and not a video as I usually do. Just after the civil wedding we went by boat to have an aperitif under a beautiful sun (it was grey 2 hours before), the atmosphere was festive, simple and very cool!

A wedding at the top! Thanks again Charles and Margaux
